##  About us
PT Sisindokom Lintasbuana was established on April 17, 1990. On February 2005, we were acquired by PT Aneka Spring Telekomindo (ASTEL), and as a member of ASTEL Group who’s established as a leading provider of telecommunication and networking Solutions Company in Indonesia. For almost 2 decades of experience, we have been providing Information Communication Technology (ICT) Solutions to the business community, and we commit to provide the most comprehensive and effective ICT solutions.

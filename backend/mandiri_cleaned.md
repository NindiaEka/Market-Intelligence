## PT. Rezki Batulicin Transportation
PT Rezki Batulicin Transportation (RBT) was established based on the Company's Deed of Establishment No.23 dated July 11, 2011. PT RBT's business activities are conducting business in the field of land transportation including transportation for goods and passengers; expeditions and warehousing; and transportation of transportation which includes transportation of goods, containers and tracking trailers; mining transportation and petroleum and transportation of plantation products.
The address of the RBT office is at Jalan Raya Batulicin No.01 RT.11 RW 03 Batulicin, Tanah Bumbu, South Kalimantan Province. PT RBT supports several mines located in Tanah Bumbu Regency, South Kalimantan including PT BIB Mine, PT MSTB Mine, PT AJE Mine, and PT KTM Contractor.
Currently PT. Rezki Batulicin Transportation has 6 units Scania, 62 units Dump Truck, 20 units Volvo Double Trailer FH16 and 2 units Water Tank, 10 unit Fuso, 30 Unit Hino ranger, 25 unit Volvo FMX 440. With the number of units, PT. Rezki Batulicin Transportation is able to meet customer needs.
